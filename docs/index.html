About CBYT

CBYT is an exact replica of the original Bitcoin protocol, launched with a fresh genesis block. It's not a fork. It's not an altcoin with tweaks. It's a clean slate, giving anyone the opportunity to relive the earliest moments of BTC — without the noise, without the middlemen, and without the missed opportunity.

The Vision

Bitcoin changed the world. CBYT seeks to preserve that raw, unadulterated potential — a true decentralized currency born again. This is not about hype. This is about principles. CBYT brings back the cypherpunk roots, where code is law and opportunity is evenly distributed. Whether you’re a miner, a developer, or a believer in financial freedom — CBYT invites you to build from the beginning.
